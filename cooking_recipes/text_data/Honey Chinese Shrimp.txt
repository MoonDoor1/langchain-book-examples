Recipe name: Honey Chinese Shrimp

Number of servings: 4

Ingredients:
  1/4 cup Honey
  16  
  1/4 cup Red wine
  2 tablespoon Sesame oil
  1.5 tablespoon Soy Sauce
  1 teaspoon Worcestershire
  2 teaspoon Sriracha Hot Chili Sauce
  1/4 cup Orange juice
  1/4 teaspoon Black pepper

Directions: Mix together all ingredients and marinate in the refrigerator for at least one hour. There are two ways to cook this dish: 1. Barbecue on a grill, occasionally basting with more marinade. 2. Remove shrimp from marinade sauce and fry in a hot non-stick frying pan. This is good served over brown or white rice with a side of steamed vegetables.
