Recipe name: Japanese Ginger Salad Dressing

Number of servings: 4

Ingredients:
  1 tablespoon Rice vinegar
  2 teaspoon Vegetable oil
  1.5 tablespoon Sesame oil
  1 tablespoon Grated fresh ginger
  1 teaspoon Soy sauce

Directions: Mix all ingredients together and let sit for at least 10 minutes before using. Some of the ingredients will settle so make sure you stir well before pouring over salad.
