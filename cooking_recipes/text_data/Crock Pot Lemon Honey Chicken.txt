Recipe name: Crock Pot Lemon Honey Chicken

Number of servings: 4

Ingredients:
  1  Roaster Chicken
  1/2 cup Water
  2  Lemon
  1/2 teaspoon Salt
  1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  1/4 cup Honey

Directions: Wash the Chicken and trim off excess fat. Put the washed chicken in a crock pot and squeeze the juice from one lemon over the chicken. Then sprinkle the salt and pepper over the chicken, and finally pour the honey over the chicken. Cook the chicken on low heat until done. After serving the chicken, squeeze some juice from the second lemon over each portion.
