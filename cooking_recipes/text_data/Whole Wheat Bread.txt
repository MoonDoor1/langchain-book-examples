Recipe name: Whole Wheat Bread

Number of servings: 10

Ingredients:
  1 1/2 cup Water
  1 1/3 tablespoon Vegetable oil
  1/4 cup Honey
  1/2 teaspoon Salt
  4 cup Whole wheat bread flour
  3 teaspoon Yeast

Directions: Lightly grease two 9x5 inch loaf pans. Blend all ingredients and then place on a large floured surface and knead for 5 or 6 minutes. Place in a covered bowl in a warm area and let it rise for about one hour. Preheat oven to 375F. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
