Recipe name: Homemade Salad Dressing

Number of servings: 16

Ingredients:
  1 teaspoon Salt
  1/4 teaspoon Black Pepper
  1/2 teaspoon Paprika
  1 pinch Dry Mustard
  1 teaspoon Sugar
  1/2 cup Olive Oil
  1/4 cup Wine Vinegar
  2 clove Clove Garlic

Directions: Dissolve sugar, salt, pepper, paprika, and dry mustard in olive oil. Stir in vinegar. Peel and mince garlic and and add to dressing.
