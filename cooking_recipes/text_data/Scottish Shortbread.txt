Recipe name: Scottish Shortbread

Number of servings: 10

Ingredients:
  4 cup Plain flour
  2 cup Butter (nothing else will do)
  1/2 cup Sugar
  1 dash Salt

Directions: Preheat oven to 350F Sift flour on a large board. Place the sugar in a separate area on the board and mix in the butter. Knead in flour adding small amounts at a time. Grease a baking sheet. Roll dough into small balls, about one inch in diameter. Flatten the balls and place on the baking sheet. Bake for 30 minutes.
