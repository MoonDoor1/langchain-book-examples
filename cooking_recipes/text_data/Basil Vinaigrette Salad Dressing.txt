Recipe name: Basil Vinaigrette Salad Dressing

Number of servings: 12

Ingredients:
  1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  1/4 teaspoon dried basil (or 1/2 tsp fresh)
  1/4 teaspoon Dried oregano (or 1/2 tsp fresh)
  1 tablespoon Olive Oil

Directions: Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Let sit at least 2 or 3 minutes before using on a salad. Refrigerate extra dressing for future use.
