Recipe name: Aioli Sauce

Number of servings: 8

Ingredients:
  3 unit Egg yolks
  4 clove Cloves garlic
  1 tablespoon Lemon juice
  1/3 teaspoon Salt
  1/4 teaspoon Tabasco
  1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  1/4 teaspoon Freshly ground black pepper
  1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  3/4 cup Extra virgin olive oil
  3 tablespoon Boiling water

Directions: Blend together the egg yolks, garlic, lemon, salt and pepper in a blender or food processor for about 2 minutes. Very slowly add the oil and mayonnaise while continuing to blend the mixture. Add the seasonings and mix together. Add the boiling water, stir well, and refrigerate for later use.
