Recipe name: Poached Salmon with Wine and Lemon

Number of servings: 4

Ingredients:
  1/4 cup Lemon juice
  1/4 cup Dry white wine
  4  Salmon fillets
  1/4 teaspoon Salt
  1/4 teaspoon Pepper
  1/4 cup Water

Directions: Use a heavy frying pan with a cover or small dutch oven to make this. Over medium heat bring the lemon juice and wine to a boil, adding in the salt and pepper, then water. Add salmon 'bad looking side' down and cover the pan. Poach salmon for 7 or 8 minutes.  The fish should easily flake when it is done. Remove from the pan and eat immediately.
