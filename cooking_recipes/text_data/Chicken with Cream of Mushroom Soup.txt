Recipe name: Chicken with Cream of Mushroom Soup

Number of servings: 2

Ingredients:
  2  Pieces chicken
  1 tablespoon Oil
  1/2 cup Pint sour cream
  1/2 teaspoon Salt
  1/4 teaspoon Pepper
  1 can Cream of mushroom soup (I use Campbell's)

Directions: Heat oil in a non-stick frying pan with medium heat. Lightly brown the chicken, about  minutes per side. Mix in the salt, pepper, and soup, then cover, and cook under low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Mix in the sour cream, cook for another 5 minutes. Make sure the chicken is done and tender before serving.
