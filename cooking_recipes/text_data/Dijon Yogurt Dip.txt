Recipe name: Dijon Yogurt Dip

Number of servings: 6

Ingredients:
  1 cup Sour cream OR Plain yogurt
  1/3 cup Mayonnaise
  1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  2 tablespoon Finely-chopped green onion
  0.5 teaspoon Seasoned salt
  1/4 teaspoon Black pepper

Directions: Mix all ingredients, cover and store in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving. Serve dip into small bowls containing and place on larger plates with cut up fresh vegetables and/or chips.
