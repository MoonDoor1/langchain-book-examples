Recipe name: Chicken Cacciatore

Number of servings: 4

Ingredients:
  2  Pieces chicken
  2  Crushed tomatoes
  2 teaspoon Sugar
  2 teaspoon Salt
  1 teaspoon Pepper
  1 teaspoon Butter
  2 tablespoon Oil
  1 teaspoon Rosemary

Directions: Wash the chicken and cook it for 5 minutes a side with butter, oil, and rosemary in a large skillet under medium heat. Then cut up the tomatoes and mix in with the cooking chicken chicken. Add the basil, sage, rosemary and parsley, sugar, salt, and pepper. Cover and cook on low to medium heat for about two hours. Check chicken for doneness before serving
