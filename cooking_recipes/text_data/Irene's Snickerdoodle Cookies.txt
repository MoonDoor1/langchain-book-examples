Recipe name: Irene's Snickerdoodle Cookies

Number of servings: 10

Ingredients:
  1 cup Shortening
  1 3/4 cup Sugar
  2  Eggs
  3 cup Flour
  2 teaspoon Cream of tartar
  1 teaspoon Soda
  1/4 teaspoon Salt
  1 dash Cinnamon sugar

Directions: preheat oven to 375F. Mix together the vegetable shortening, sugar and eggs in a small mixing bowl. Sift together flour and mix well with the cream of tartar, soda and salt. Mix together both mixtures and roll into  balls about one inch in diameter and roll in cinnamon and  sugar. Put on a lightly greased baking sheet, leaving about 1 1/2 inches between the cookies. Bake from 10 to 12 minutes until done.
