Recipe name: Indonesian Barbecue Sauce

Number of servings: 12

Ingredients:
  1/2 cup Corn syrup (dark)
  1/4 cup Creamy peanut butter
  1/4 cup Soy sauce
  1/4 cup Cider vinegar
  1/4 cup Sliced green onions
  4 clove garlic
  1 teaspoon Clove 
  2 teaspoon Ginger
  1 teaspoon Crushed dried red pepper

Directions: Mix together all ingredients and let sit for at least one hour before using. Refrigerate or freeze any unused barbecue sauce for future use.
